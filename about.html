Academy Story

Nine Seasons In The Making

Sol Addis Football Academy began on a single rented pitch in 2016 with 30 children and one coach. What started as a weekend program has grown into a full-time academy serving hundreds of players across seven age groups.

Today the academy operates its own training grounds, a licensed coaching staff, and a structured pathway that has sent players into regional and professional trials across Ethiopia and beyond.

We remain a football-first, character-first academy — building players who compete with discipline and represent their community with pride.

Our Mission

To develop technically excellent, mentally resilient footballers through structured, age-appropriate coaching.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's leading pathway academy, producing players who compete at the highest level of the game.

Our Values

Discipline, respect, teamwork and relentless effort — on the pitch and in the classroom.

Our Philosophy

How We Coach

Player development is treated as a long-term craft, not a quick result.

01

Technique Before Tactics

Younger squads focus on ball mastery before structured tactical concepts are introduced.

02

Small-Sided, High Reps

Training favors small-sided games that maximize touches and decision-making.

03

Individual Development Plans

Every player is tracked against personal technical and physical benchmarks each season.

04

Character On & Off The Ball

Coaches are held to the same standard of discipline and respect expected of players.
